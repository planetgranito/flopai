Our Projects

Our AI Design Helper is a long-term, transformative project aimed at revolutionizing habitat design by making high-quality architectural planning accessible to everyone. While not a short-term commercial venture, its ultimate goal is to develop a system capable of fully automating design through AI or future AGI systems. Along the way, we are delivering product lines that enhance current workflows, easing the design process with today’s technology. By automating repetitive tasks, optimizing layouts, and providing real-time suggestions, it boosts efficiency, reduces errors, and eliminates unnecessary losses. This shift will not only streamline the work of architects and designers but also democratize design, making professional-quality spaces achievable for all.

Our AI-augmented architectural material procurement (sourcing solutions ) system empowers anyone T—whether architects, designers, or homeowners—to effortlessly access, source, and integrate the perfect materials for any project. By cutting sourcing time by 10x and reducing costs by 20-30%, it streamlines decision-making and eliminates inefficiencies. Designed for seamless integration with existing workflows, including BIM systems, it makes finding and fitting the right materials faster and smarter. While AI currently handles discovery and selection, human expertise ensures verification and logistics—but in the future, AI will make the entire construction and design process effortless. With real-time pricing on our roadmap, we’re redefining how spaces are built, ensuring that imagination—not complexity—is the only limit.

Then there’s our AI Mirror, which is changing the game in fashion retail. This smart device analyzes a user’s preferences and body data to offer personalized fashion advice and health insights. It’s more than just technology—it’s a way to make shopping feel more tailored and engaging, benefiting both customers and retailers alike

What Sets Us Apart

Flop AI takes a different approach in the ever-evolving AI landscape. We don’t chase broad, generic solutions that bigger companies can easily replicate. Instead, we focus exclusively on niche industries—architecture, fashion retail, and construction—where AI adoption is still fragmented and real impact can be made. Our goal is to develop specialized tools that solve distinct, overlooked challenges, making AI truly useful for these sectors.

AI development today is unpredictable and heavily dependent on base models like LLMs, which are costly to train, fine-tune, and maintain. Betting everything on a single project is a high-risk move. That’s why we embrace an agile approach inspired by Real Options Theory and lean startup principles. We run parallel MVPs, experiment rapidly, and pivot when necessary to stay ahead in a field where technology evolves unpredictably. This flexibility allows us to deliver real, working solutions rather than getting stuck in endless research cycles.

At Flop AI, speed and adaptability define our work We don’t just build for the sake of innovation—we create practical, intuitive tools that solve real problems. It’s not about having the flashiest AI features; it’s about ensuring that technology actually enhances workflows, reduces inefficiencies, and empowers users.
